Dwarven Abilities

Mighty Deeds of Arms :
 
Lucky Weapon:
 
Shield bash (d14)
 
  • Infravision 60'
  • Underground Skills
    • Smell Gold/Gems
      • Strong concentration within 100'
      • Smaller concentrations, down to a single coin, as low as 40' (for a single coin or gem)
    • Find Construction: Bonus equal to Class Level
      • Detecting Traps
      • Detecting Slanting Passages
      • Detecting Shifting Walls
      • Detecting New Construction

Halfling Abilities

Stealth Bonus :
 
  • Two-weapon fighting
    • Attack Die: d16 + d16
    • Can fight with two equal sized weapons, such as two handaxes or two short swords
    • Scores a Critical and Automatic hit on a natural 16
    • Fumbles only when both dice come up a 1
    • If AGI >16, use normal two-weapon fighting rules
  • Infravision 30'
  • Good Luck Charm
    • Double the bonus of a Luck check
      • For every 1 point of Luck expended, gain a +2 to your roll
    • Recover lost Luck
      • Luck score is restored each night by a number of points equal to level
      • Luck score cannot be taken past natural maximum
    • Expend Luck for Allies
      • Ally must be nearby and visible
      • Can act out of Initiative order to burn Luck and apply to Ally's rolls
      • Luck Modifier can apply to any roll made by an Ally
      • This trait applies to only one halfling in a party. More halflings do not make the party more lucky.

Morlock Abilities

Acute Senses Check :
 
  • Claw Fighting
    • Must fight with both claws
    • Each claw gets its own attack die: d16
    • Only the Primary Claw scores a Critical and Automatic hit on a natural 16
    • Only the Primary Claw fumbles on a 1
    • Claw damage increases with level
    • If AGI >16, use normal two-weapon fighting rules
  • Burrow
    • Can only burrow through earth
    • Can burrow at a movement rate of 20'
    • Tunnel made is only for use by Morlocks
  • Underground Skills
    • Smell Gold/Gems
      • Strong concentration within 100'
      • Smaller concentrations, down to a single coin, as low as 40' (for a single coin or gem)
    • Find Construction: Bonus equal to Class Level
      • Detecting Traps
      • Detecting Slanting Passages
      • Detecting Shifting Walls
      • Detecting New Construction
  • Blind Luck
    • Can detect vibrations in the earth (since they are effectively blind)
    • Immune to visual illusions or anything dependent on sight
    • Acute Senses
      • Greater sense of smell, sound and vibrations
      • Once per session, can roll a Luck Check to automatically dodge an incoming surprise attack, notice an important clue, avoid a trap, or anything else the player can think of and the judge accepts.
    • Twice as difficult to catch by surprise in underground environments

The first time you attempt to cast a non-divine spell, you need to roll your Mercurial Magic check along with your Manifestation check. There are text boxes to enter these effects for each spell. For players attempting to cast divine spells for the first time, you need to roll a Manifestation check and enter the information into the spell.

Add any equipment, effects or items here that give a benefit to your Armor Class (AC). This includes Armor, shields and any magical items or spells. Use the checkboxes to indicate whether each row is is worn/equipped (meaning it applies to your normal AC score) and whether the row also provides a benefit to your unArmored AC score (such as magical items or in some cases a shield). If you have a class feature that also boosts your unArmored AC then select that here too.
