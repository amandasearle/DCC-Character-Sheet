Use the checkboxes to indicate whether each row is is worn/equipped (meaning it applies to your normal AC score) and whether the row also provides a benefit to your unArmored AC score (such as magical items or in some cases a shield). If you have a class feature that also boosts your unArmored AC then select that here too.
